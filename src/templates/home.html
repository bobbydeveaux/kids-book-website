Amazing Donkey Facts

Discover fascinating insights about these remarkable animals.

  • Ancient Companions: Donkeys have been working alongside humans for over 5,000 years.
  • Incredible Memory: They can recognize a person or animal after 25 years of separation.
  • Desert Adapted: Originally from arid regions, they're perfectly suited for dry climates.
  • Intelligent Guards: They naturally protect livestock from predators like coyotes.
