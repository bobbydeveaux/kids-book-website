Donkey Breeds

Explore the diverse world of donkey breeds, from miniature to mammoth. Each breed has unique characteristics, history, and care requirements that make them special companions.

Available Donkey Breeds

Miniature Mediterranean

32-34 inches 200-350 lbs

Charming small donkeys from Mediterranean islands, known for their gentle temperament and friendly nature. Perfect companions for small farms and therapy work.

Learn More

Standard Donkey

36-48 inches 400-700 lbs

The classic donkey breed found worldwide, prized for their intelligence, sure-footedness, and reliable work ethic. Excellent for farm work and companionship.

Learn More

Mammoth Donkey

56+ inches 900-1,300 lbs

The largest donkey breed, originally developed in America for mule production. Impressive size with gentle giants' temperament, perfect for large farms and breeding.

Learn More

Poitou Donkey

52-56 inches 700-950 lbs

Rare French breed famous for their distinctive long, shaggy coat called a "cadanette." Historic breed prized for producing high-quality mules, now a conservation priority.

Learn More
